How to play

Once logged in, click the "Play a Scenario" to choose from the available scenarios.

Select the scenario you wish to play and then chose a solution for each problem.

Solving each problem rewards a token, which you may use to redeem prizes at the store!


To visit the store, click on the store page button.

Once in the store, select from a category and then chose the item you want to buy!

Once purchased, you can switch between themes or user pictures in the store page by clicking the paint brush picture.


You can also switch between purchased themes and user pictures in the profile page.

From the profile page you can also change the font size, and also upload a picture from your device!


To navigate to a page you want to go to, click on the button of your choice near the top of the screen.

In order to log out of the game when finished, press the logout button in the top of your screen.
